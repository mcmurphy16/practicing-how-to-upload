The Best Movies According to Mrunal

My top 3 movies of all-time

12 Angry Men

I enjoy watching those movies, which involves legal plot, and the ones which allows the audience to think. This movie is a perfect blend of the two features.

Forrest Gump

This movie has lot of entertaining and fun moments, a good thing about this movie is - it allows the audience to look at life as an unpredictable journey.

Erin Brockovich

A truly motivational movie, based on the life of Erin Brockovich, which highlights the importance of self-spirit in hard times to accomplish a task related to social service.


P.S. Whosoever is a fan of Julia Roberts, should definitely consider to watch Erin Brockovich.
